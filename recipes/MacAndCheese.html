Mac and Cheese

This Mac and Cheese recipe is the classic.

The staple of the american diet. The original, the greatest, the most wonderful, the MAC.

Ingredients

  • 1 Box of Kraft (tm) Mac and Cheese
  • 1/2 a stick of butter
  • 1 Cup of milk
  • Some water
  • Handful of shredded cheese
  • Spices of your choosing and Sriracha

Steps

  1. Eyeball water to boil, need enough to cover the noodles but the more you use the longer it takes.
  2. Once at a boil, place Mac and Cheese in noodles in pot, don't add flavor pack
  3. After 6-7 minutes, sift out water, now things have to move quickly to ensure meltage
  4. Start by adding the butter, cheese packed, and a handful of shredded cheese
  5. Start stiring until semi melted, then add milk
  6. Once mostly melted, add Sriracha and the spices of your choice to taste
