Fried Tofu

This Fried Tofu recipe will result in a flavorful and crunchy snack for any occasion

This recipe uses an airfryer for low effort but using oil in a pan would also work. Tofu is tough to make at times as its inital texture isn't very delecitble.

Ingredients

  • Extra Firm Tofu
  • Olive Oil
  • Onion and Garlic Powder
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Corn Starch

Steps

  1. Squish liquid out of tofu by placing on tofu on a plate and applied weight such as books on top of it.
  2. After 10 minutes of letting the liquid out, chop into small cubes (1" x 1" max)
  3. Coat Tofu cubes in a small amount of olive oil (more if using pan fried method)
  4. Coat in salt, pepper, onion powder, and garlic powder.
  5. Apply a layer of corn starch so the whole cube is completely covered
  6. Place coated cubes in airfryer, cook for 5 minutes at 400 degrees F and then shake.
  7. Cook again for 5 minutes.
